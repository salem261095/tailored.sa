Agency brief

Tailored.sa presents itself as “وكالة مُحاك الإبداعية” (Tailored Creative Agency), a Saudi creative/marketing agency focused on helping brands build stronger digital presence. Its positioning is around brand-building, content, marketing strategy, UX writing, and web development. The core homepage message is about creating an impactful brand that builds trust, increases presence, and multiplies success.

The brand story on the About page is strongly narrative and heritage-driven. It connects the agency identity to an Ahsa family background in traditional craftsmanship such as tailoring bishts and goldsmithing, then maps that craft metaphor into branding, writing, and creative strategy. It also says the agency emerged during the COVID-era shift toward digital business needs in the Arab region, and that it started from the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia.

What the agency appears to offer

The website explicitly lists these service areas: brand strategy/branding, social media content production, marketing strategy, UX writing, and web development. The homepage also frames its value around audience analysis, visual identity, brand-message consistency, and promotional creativity.

Current website structure

The visible top-level navigation is very simple: Home, Blog, About, Contact. The footer repeats the brand summary and social links, and the site also includes a newsletter subscription area.

Homepage layout and sections

The homepage structure appears to be:

Header / Navbar — logo plus links to Home, Blog, About, Contact.
Hero section — strong headline, short supporting statement, and a primary CTA to contact the agency.
Client / brand logos section — a strip of client or partner logos.
Services intro (“خدماتنا”) — short benefit-led blocks about brand consistency, audience understanding, visual identity, and promotional creativity.
Detailed offerings (“ماذا نقدم؟”) — service cards or content blocks for Branding, Social Media Content, Marketing Strategy, UX Writing, and Web Development.
Mid-page CTA section — “لا تكتفي بالمنافسة… بل تصدّر” with a free consultation call to action.
Digital impact / benefits section — four numbered outcomes: data-informed decisions, SEO visibility, targeted campaigns, and evergreen content.
Latest articles section — recent blog posts preview.
Newsletter signup — email subscription form.
Footer — short agency summary, social links, copyright.

Other pages

The About page is mostly a long-form brand story with one CTA. It is content-led rather than section-heavy.

The Blog page is essentially a posts listing page with pagination. Topics are centered on branding, archetypes, marketing, and legal-sector marketing content.

The Contact page contains a simple contact heading, service inquiry framing, WhatsApp, phone, email, physical location in Dammam, and a submission form.

Coding-oriented takeaway for your redesign

From an information-architecture perspective, the current site is a small agency brochure site with 4 core pages and a homepage that does most of the selling. So when you rebuild in Next.js, the main content model you need to preserve is: brand story + services + proof/logos + outcomes + blog + contact funnel.
